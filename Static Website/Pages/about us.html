Our Story


We created the PakistanTimes.com with the user's perspective in mind. We wanted to offer a platform that would allow readers to keep in touch with the daily national and internatinal news updates. We keep it simple, so users can easily access the current national and international affairs with a click.

Our Goals


Our key goals are to keep our readers in touch with the daily national and international affairs. Providing them daily news updates from all around the world. One of our key goals is to hightlight our national heroes, who dedicated their lives for the betterment of our dear motherland. And these heroes include Scientists, Doctors, Humanitarians, Philanthropists, Media Personalities, Sportsmen, Actors etc. So that, our young generation can learn from them, motivate themselves from the working of these heroes, and make them thier role models.

Our Readers Mean the World


At PakistanTimes.com, we strive to provide exactly what our readers are looking for. A huge part of our brainstorming process is looking at our client feedback to make sure you're well taken care of.
